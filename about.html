Raahi - Your Road Trip Companion
Raahi is location based road trip companion, which allows users to find different services like Accomodation, Refreshments, Hospital and Garages near by in radius of 15 km. Raahi is a project developed by two budding IT Engineers. We aim to solve the problem of long time road travellers who find it difficult to find nearby washroom usable facilities, refreshment and accomodation facilities. This app also helps local businesses and joints to revolutionize their business by registering to our app.
